Strangeball
by Stefano 'Lord Strangiato' Pinti (https://github.com/Rolpa)

This map is HORSE BALONEY!

Grab the 'ball' and smash the opposing team's barrier. 
Use their severed head to win the round! 
A new 'ball' is spawned 30 seconds after a thrown one hits the ground.
The pools next to spawn will regenerate your health.
