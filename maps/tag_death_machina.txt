tag_death_machina

Map by MistaX88 [COF]

An ambitious map inspired by "Eh!! The Reaper's" map de_ethics_v2 and countless maps made by the legendary Nipper.

Story:
In an effort to compete with Aperture Science the lesser known Iris Laboratories needed to gather test subjects
quickly. To do so they hired a mercenary group under false pretences to trap them in the facility. They then called up the
regional Counter-Terrorism forces to take care of the threat, only to secretly trap them in the facility as well.

Both parties soon realized they were stuck in the facility fighting over hologram hostages. Amongst many of the
things being experimented with, Iris was perfecting their breakthrough medical systems that would revive
people even after being shot up; even in the head! The early success of this system has allowed the team
at Iris to keep the mercenaries and counter-terrorism forces indefinitely as they repeatedly force them
to battle each other over holograms and revive them to have to fight all over again the next night.

Instructions:
Each team must complete a deadly obstacle course before getting onto the battlefield.
There are 6 hostages on the map but they are inside of their own rescue zone, meaning a CT must only "tag out" the
hostages. Only 1 player from each team may enter the "AWP Door" to skip past most of the obstacle course and
knife fight the enemy player to see who gets the one AWP on the map. To force a knife fight, a hole in the
floor must be broken open (taking 4 right-click stabs with the knife). I wouldn't recommend teaming up
with the enemy player and cooperating to open up the floor because remember: There can be only one!

This map has a few secrets!
