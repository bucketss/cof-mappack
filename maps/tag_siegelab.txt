tag_siegelab

Map by MistaX88 [COF]

Play COF! clancof.net

---------------------

Another map using my "tag" type hostage game
mode. "Tag" is a hostage rescue map in which
the hostages spawn inside a rescue zone. This
means that they must only be "tagged out" to
be rescued. This map has another twist in
that it tries to mimic a "siege type" game
mode with 1-way barriers. All Terrorists
spawn inside the laboratory with the
hostages and can't leave the building. The
Counter-Terrorists spawn on either side of
the building. Once they enter they also cannot
leave.
