de_pophouse_beta

Map by MistaX88 [COF]

Using default textures along with a few custom textures by MistaX88.

This map has a single bombsite that is designed for quick and fun play with 10-12 players or less in the spirit of a Demolition map like in CS:GO.
However, larger servers have been taken into consideration and there are still 32 spawn points on the map. Gun selection is intentionally limited
so doors will open in each spawn depending on the number of players on each team! This is the beta version of the map, please let me know if you
run into any problems! Just look up this map on gamebanana.com or 17buddies.net and leave feedback there!
