de_msx1

Map, custom sounds, and custom textures by MistaX88 [COF]

Play COF! clancof.net

A map with a simple aesthetic.

Counter-Terrorists start directly on top of one bombsite with a short route to the other
with a good sniper vantage point right in spawn that also offers a quick rotation to either.

The Terrorists have some potential flanking options to counter this initial advantage,
including the "long way" around the map that Counter-Terrorists should be sure not to
leave exposed.

I hope everyone has fun on this one!
