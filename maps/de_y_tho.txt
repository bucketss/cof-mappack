Map by MistaX88 [COF]

---------------------

The local Youth Training Help Organization ("YTHO") is under attack by
a terrorist group formed by local members of the community who keep
shouting something about missing people. The special YTHO security force
has been dispatched to stop these criminals from destroying a totally
harmless and innocent youth center that simply wishes to foster a healthy
community.
